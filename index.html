Overview

What is AetherSpice?

AetherSpice ($AETHR) is the native token of the Cosmo's AI project — a creative AI Game Master that can turn ideas into fully playable campaigns, worlds, and narrative systems on the fly.

Built by MagicianzCardstock LLC, AetherSpice is designed as the connective tissue between AI-powered storytelling, onchain ownership of digital creations, and experimental player rewards.

The early focus is on slow, organic growth with a community of curious explorers, builders, and players who want to experiment with AI-native games and shared universes.

Cosmo's AI

Cosmo's AI — An AI Game Master on Virtuals

Cosmo's AI is built on Virtuals Protocol. It acts like a 24/7 Game Master that can:

  • Turn a single sentence into a full campaign or scenario.
  • Create NPCs, locations, rules, and story arcs dynamically.
  • Adapt to your playstyle over time.
  • Experiment with shared worlds between multiple creators.

AetherSpice ties into this by eventually powering things like:

  • Access to premium or extended AI features.
  • Reward loops for players, storytellers, and world-builders.
  • Onchain records of stories, worlds, and digital artifacts.

Roadmap

Lightweight Roadmap

This roadmap is intentionally minimal and flexible. The goal is to experiment, listen to the community, and build in public.

  • Phase 1 — Launch & Foundations Deploy token on Base, basic website, and Cosmo's AI prototype on Virtuals. Begin sharing the concept with a small, curious community.
  • Phase 2 — AI & World-Building Improve Cosmo's AI interactions, refine prompts, and test AI-powered campaigns with early players and storytellers.
  • Phase 3 — Utility Experiments Explore simple token integrations such as access gates, rewards, or collectible story artifacts tied to AI-generated worlds.
